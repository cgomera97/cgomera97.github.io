Ozzie.
Typeface Design
“Ozzie.” is a typeface I developed based off of the structure of Cooper STD.

To make the typeface my own, after several iterations, I took a pen and outlined it with a single swirling line. Its hand-drawn thin swirl patterns are inspired by the idea of bringing femininity and lightness to the very heavy and bold Cooper STD.

A poster, type specimen book, and promotional poster were the result.
