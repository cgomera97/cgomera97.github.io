HexBox
Brand and business solution for NYC Brujas
As a proposed solution to a lack of authentic goods and availability of knowledge on Brujeria, HexBox is a modern community garden located in a Brooklyn alley committed to providing garden spaces, quality produce, and authentic goods to the Bruja community.

I developed a story, logo, branding guidlines, concept mockups, business cards, pins, and sample products to sell my brand.
