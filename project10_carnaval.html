Afro-Caribbean Carnaval
Custom Event Poster Design
Afro-Caribbean Carnaval is an annual spring event I organized at the Maryland Institute College of Art with the Center for Identity & Inclusion and the Hispanic and Black Student Unions. It celebrates the African diaspora in Latin and Caribbean Americas while hosting craft art sales, performers, and music

For the poster I chose to highlight the craft aspect of the event and create a papercut piece.

The colors and cut illustrations highlight shared symbols of Caribbean culture.
