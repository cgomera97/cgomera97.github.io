A Typographic Dialogue
Experimental Book
This experimental book is an exploration in the texts from Frank Chimero’s “Gifts & Giving” and Oscar Wilde’s “A Picture of Dorian Gray”.

The 32 pg book visually represents a conversation between the two, with different compositions on each spread.

The binding of the book reflects the idea of art as a handmade gift to be unwrapped, as proposed by Chimero.
