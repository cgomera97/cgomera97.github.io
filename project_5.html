Roots & Raíces
Arts & Music Festival for Baltimore City Immigrants
Starting as a Undergrad thesis project from MICA's Valeria Fuentes, Roots & Raíces Fest is a celebration of Baltimore's immigrants, with a focus on Latin immigrant rights.

I was asked to create a logo and design the wordpress site for this festival event. I also developed a pattern.
