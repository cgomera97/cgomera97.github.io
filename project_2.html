Don't Forget to Love Yourself
Hand-drawn Lettering
For this Project, I workshopped with a hand-lettering professional to create a phrase inspired by Valentine's Day.

I chose to focus on self-love and develop my own style of brush/bubble letters. The final product was a vectorized version of my drawing made into a card.
